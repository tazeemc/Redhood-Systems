- Average trader monitors 20+ sources
- FOMO-driven decision making
- Growing demand for curation/aggregation
- "Spotify for market intelligence" opportunity

### Risk Factors

**Market Risks:**
- Retail trading volume cyclical (bull market dependent)
- Regulatory changes (pattern day trader rules, options restrictions)
- Competition from free tools (subsidized by order flow)

**Technical Risks:**
- API rate limits (X, Telegram)
- LLM costs at scale
- Data quality/reliability

**Go-to-Market Risks:**
- User acquisition cost in crowded market
- Retention if not immediately valuable
- Competing with "free" (manual aggregation)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Build for serious traders (less price-sensitive)
- Focus on time savings (ROI calculation)
- Freemium model for trial/conversion
